How to Make a Delicious Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or your favorite)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly ( grape, strawberry, or your choice)
  • A knife

Steps:

  1. Take one slice of bread and spread a generous amount of peanut butter on top.
  2. Take the second slice of bread and spread jelly on one side.
  3. Place the second slice of bread, jelly-side down, on top of the peanut butter to make a sandwich.
  4. If desired, cut the sandwich in half diagonally or into four quarters for easier eating.

Tip:

For an extra special treat, try using different types of bread, peanut butter (e.g., crunchy vs. smooth), and jelly combinations to find your perfect flavor combination!
